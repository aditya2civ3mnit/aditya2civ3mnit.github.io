## Section 2: Key Features
### Title
Everything You Need in One Workflow

### Feature Blocks
1. Setup Canvas
Build reusable setup playbooks with nested steps and If/Else logic.

2. Dynamic Checklists
Your checklist auto-renders from setup JSON and tracks completion in real time.

3. Media Evidence
Attach screenshots and videos per setup segment so every decision has proof.

4. Trade Replay
Play trades slide-by-slide with checklist state and media context.

5. Post-Trade Review
Log outcome, close reason, P/L, and review notes in structured format.

6. Monthly Analytics
See green/red day distribution and weekly performance summaries.

7. Risk and Lot Calculator
Use the floating calculator to size positions from account risk and stop distance.
